GET CONNECTED

Life is better together, and there are a few ways that we encourage everyone to connect at First Presbyterian.

Sunday Worship

Each Sunday morning we gather to worship, learn, and connect. These gatherings are an important opportunity for us to grow closer to God and to one another. No matter where you are in life, there's a place for you here on Sunday.

Events

There is always something going on at our church. As an active, growing, intergenerational church there are different events happening for people of all ages and stages of life. Check out our list of upcoming events and we'll see you soon.

Small Groups

Joining a small group provides a place for you to get to know other people and walk through the journey of life together. Groups are a place to celebrate the good times, support each other through the hard times, and to seek God together.

Serving

Serving others is one of the best ways that we are able to demonstrate the love of Christ to others. From welcoming people into the building to participating in events serving our region, we have regular opportunities for serving here at church and in the community.
